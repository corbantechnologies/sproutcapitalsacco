# Sprout SACCO: Member’s User Guide

Welcome to your Sprout SACCO Member Portal! This guide will help you navigate your dashboard and manage your financial growth.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. Account Activation
If your Admin has just registered you, you will receive an invitation. 
*   **Action:** Click the link in your email to set your password.
*   **Login:** Use your **Member Number** and the password you created to access the portal.

### 2. Your Dashboard
The dashboard provides a bird's-eye view of your financial health:
*   **Total Savings:** The combined balance across all your savings accounts.
*   **Outstanding Loans:** Total amount you currently owe.
*   **Recent Transactions:** A quick log of your latest deposits or repayments.

---

## 💰 Managing Your Savings

### Viewing Your Accounts
Navigate to the **Savings** section to see a list of all your accounts (e.g., Main Savings, Share Capital).
*   **Transactions:** Click on any account to see a full history of deposits and withdrawals.
*   **Interest:** If your savings type earns interest, you'll see those credits listed here.

---

## 📈 Loans & Credit

### The Loan Application Journey
1.  **Select Product:** Choose your loan type (Emergency, Development, etc.).
2.  **Calculate & Apply:** Use the calculator to preview your interest and schedule.
3.  **Amendment Phase:** If the SACCO Admin needs to adjust your requested amount, you will see an "Amended" status. You can **Accept** the new terms or **Cancel** the application.
4.  **Guarantors:** If your loan isn't fully covered by your savings, you must invite other members to guarantee you. They will receive an invitation to accept or decline.
5.  **Final Submission:** Once you are 100% guaranteed, click **Submit** for the Admin's final approval.

### Repayments & Tracking
Sprout makes it easy to stay on track:
*   **Balance Updates:** Every payment you make is reconciled with the SACCO's books and your portal balance is typically updated by the **end of the day**.
*   **Repayment Schedule:** View your personalized calendar of upcoming payments to avoid penalties.

*   **Statement:** Download a full loan statement at any time to see your progress.


---

## 📄 Reports & Profile

### Financial Statements
Need a statement for personal records or visa applications?
*   Go to **Reports** to download PDF statements for any period.

### Profile & Security
Keep your information up to date in **Settings**.
*   Update your contact details.
*   Change your password regularly for security.

---

> [!TIP]
> **Saving regularly is key!** Most SACCOs base your loan eligibility on your savings history. The more you save, the more you can borrow.
